The Ocean Reporting Tool is a web based, report centric tool for Ocean Planners. It provides high-level summary statistics and interpretive analysis for the Southeastern United States.

Getting Started
Choose a geographic area to generate a report:

  Click KNOWN AREAS to select a report from the list of pre-defined areas

  Click DRAW AREA to use the map to generate a report for a custom area

  Click RESET to clear your current selection and report

Area selections initially display general report information. To view specific subject information, you may click on the tabs for energy and minerals, natural resources and conservation, marine transportation and infrastructure, and economics and commerce.

Export options for a report include:
  • Printing
  • Sharing
